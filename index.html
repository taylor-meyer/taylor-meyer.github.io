710

"710" or "7!0" is an Android app developed with Dart & Flutter. 710 allows users to set up road trips of any distance and the price of fuel will be split between all those riding in the vehicle. Users can login with their Google email and be authenticated through Firebase. The fuel cost per gallon is an average per county and is pulled from the Internet and uploaded to Firebase using Python.
