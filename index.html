710

GitHub

"710" or "7!0" is an Android app developed with Dart & Flutter. 710 allows users to set up road trips of any distance and the price of fuel will be split between all those riding in the vehicle. Users can login with their Google email and be authenticated through Firebase. The fuel cost per gallon is an average per county and is pulled from the Internet and uploaded to Firebase using Python.

N-Queens Problem Solver

GitHub

The N Queens Problem is the problem of placing N queens on a chessboard of NxN rows & columns such that any queen cannot attack any other queen. This Python script accepts a user inputted N, and k states for use with a genetic algorithm. The genetic algorithm randomely generate k solutions to the problem. The k states are given a score from a fitness function, then spliced together and mutated. If the new states score lower on the fitness function, they are thrown out. The splicing and mutating continue until the answer is found. With an N of 8 (a standard 8x8 chessboard), the solution is found in less than one second. A solution of N of 50 can be solved between two and four minutes.
